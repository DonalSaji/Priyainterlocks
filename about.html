About Us

Welcome to Priya Interlock

PRIYA Interlock Industry has been a trusted name in the construction materials industry since its inception in 2010. Located in Manya, Post Ullodi, Kasaragod, we have steadily grown to become a leading manufacturer and installer of high-quality interlocking stones and hollow bricks.

2010: Our journey began with a focus on producing top-tier interlocking stones designed to meet the diverse needs of domestic, commercial, and heavy traffic areas. Over the years, we have expanded our product range and enhanced our manufacturing processes to ensure the highest quality and customer satisfaction.

2015: Building on our success, we established a Hollow Bricks factory in Edaneer, Kasaragod, to provide robust, lightweight, and energy-efficient building materials. This expansion allowed us to cater to a broader range of construction needs, further solidifying our reputation in the industry.
